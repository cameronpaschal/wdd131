Home page

Welcome to Music by Lucas Meet Lucas, a 20-year-old Saratoga Springs Utah native and a dedicated music creator with over 5 years of solo crafting in his room. From writing and producing to mixing and mastering, he's a one-man band shaping every note. Lucas doesn't just create music; he curates experiences. His passion lies in making you feel something, encouraging you to explore new realms when you tune into his tracks. The music will take you on a journey. In addition to music, he also specializes in social media marketing and video production. He has experience working on a media team, filming, and editing videos of himself and others. Experience some of his latest music and media projects below and enjoy the ride.

Marketing

Lucas has extensive experience marketing through his own efforts to gain following for his music. He is currently working with Strive to Be creating content for them to promote the newest youth album from the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. Lately, his work has focused on creating videos of college-aged young adults listening to the songs and describing how it has impacted their day. For any marketing inquiries, please see the contact page to connect with Lucas.

Connect

If you want to connect with Lucas for inquiries about potential marketing jobs, media projects, collaborations, or other associated interests the best way to reach him is through his email or through Instagram. Please be ready to provide, your name, company (if applicable), and how he can help in the message. He also stays up to date on all the main social media platforms check those out as well.
